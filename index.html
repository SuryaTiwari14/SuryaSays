Surya Says Game Instructions

Welcome to the Simon Says game! Here are the instructions:

  1. Surya will show a sequence of colors with sounds.
  2. Your task is to repeat the sequence exactly as shown.
  3. If you make a mistake or repeat the sequence incorrectly, you lose.
  4. The game will become progressively more challenging as you continue.
  5. Pay attention and try to beat your high score!

Are you ready to play? Have fun and enjoy the game!
